Pre-Screening

The goal of this "mock interview" page is to give you an idea of what I'm like to work with, the type of organization I'm looking to serve, and the value I can bring to your organization. If you'd like to talk further, feel free to reach me using the information on the "Contact" page.


So, tell me about yourself...

In short, I'm a creative professional who uses multimedia to achieve business objectives. I thoroughly enjoy collaborating with teams and working closely with people of all levels of an organization to find creative solutions that drive performance. In terms of personality, I'm somewhat reserved as I prefer to listen to others more than speak. However, I'm comfortable presenting in front of groups and I prefer to keep the mood enjoyable while getting things accomplished.


What are you looking for in an employer?

Aside from the obvious perks like competitive compensation and a safe working environment, I'm primarily looking for an employer who embraces innovation when solving communications challenges. Research (and my experience) shows that organizations fall short of their true potential when decision-makers are only interested in doing what they've already done before. I'm all for using tried-and-true methods to solve problems, but I'm at my best and happiest when tasked to find breakthrough solutions for stubborn business challenges.


Which are you more interested in: full-time or contract employment?

More than anything, I'm interested in a long-term relationship. I'm confident that once I'm given the opportunity to support your organization my work will lead to a long-term relationship in either capacity.
